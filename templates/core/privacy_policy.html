Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 29, 2026

At Wanderlust, accessible from our website, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Wanderlust and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

When you register for an account, make a tour booking, or send a support request through our contact form, we collect personal details including but not limited to:

  • Personal Identifier details (Full name, passport numbers, age, gender)
  • Contact details (Email, phone number, physical address)
  • Travel booking specifications and transaction history

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect in various ways, including to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our travel booking website
  • Process your travel bookings and coordinate reservations with hotels and airline charters
  • Improve, personalize, and expand our services and tour catalog
  • Communicate with you for customer support, service alerts, and newsletter promotions
  • Prevent fraudulent activities and enhance payment security

3. Data Retention and Protection

We retain collected information for as long as necessary to provide you with your requested service. What data we store, we will protect within commercially acceptable means to prevent loss and theft, as well as unauthorized access, disclosure, copying, use, or modification.

4. Cookies and Web Beacons

Like any other website, Wanderlust uses 'cookies'. These cookies are used to store information including visitors' preferences, and the pages on the website that the visitor accessed or visited. The information is used to optimize the users' experience by customizing our web page content based on visitors' browser type and/or other information.
