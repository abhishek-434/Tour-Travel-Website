Who We Are

Designing Custom Expeditions Since 2012

Wanderlust was born out of a simple realization: standard group packages fail to capture the true soul of a destination. We set out to build a travel agency that listens, understands, and designs experiences specifically for each individual guest.

Our relationships with elite luxury resorts, native private charters, and professional guides give us access to hidden corridors and itineraries that remain closed to the general public. From luxury overwater bungalows in Bora Bora to high-altitude treks in the Himalayas, we provide seamless, end-to-end luxury management.
